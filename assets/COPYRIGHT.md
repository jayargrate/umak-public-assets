## ⚠️ Unauthorized Use is Strictly Prohibited

Any reproduction, redistribution, modification, or use of these assets — in whole or in part — **without prior written authorization** from the University of Makati constitutes an **offense under copyright and trademark law**.

Unauthorized use includes but is not limited to:

- Modifying, distorting, or altering any Mark in any way
- Misrepresenting UMak affiliation or implying false sponsorship
- Using the Marks on social media handles, domains, or digital accounts without CIC authorization
- Using AI tools to generate, reconstruct, or approximate any UMak Mark
- Uploading or publishing official UMak digital asset packages to public repositories, CDNs, or file-sharing platforms without prior written authorization from CIC
- Hotlinking assets directly from UMak-controlled servers or repositories from external platforms

Violations are subject to civil and criminal liability under **RA No. 8293**, as amended, including administrative sanctions, issuance of a Cease-and-Desist Notice, and legal action initiated by the University through its legal counsel.

## 🚨 Digital Impersonation & Phishing is a Criminal Offense

Cloning, forking, or using these assets to create **phishing sites**, fraudulent portals, look-alike domains, impersonation pages, or any digital property that falsely implies affiliation with the University of Makati is a **criminal offense** punishable under:

- **Republic Act No. 10175** — Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012
  - Section 4(a)(1) — Illegal Access
  - Section 6 — All offenses under the Revised Penal Code, as amended, and special laws committed by, through, and with the use of information and communications technologies

This includes registering domains or app names containing UMak Marks or confusingly similar variations (e.g., `umak-ph.com`, `universityofmakati.net`, `umak-portal.app`).

Offenders will be reported to the **Cybercrime Investigation and Coordinating Center (CICC)** and appropriate law enforcement authorities. UMak reserves the right to pursue all available civil and criminal remedies.

> *Reference: UMak Trademark Brand Usage Policy, Sections 6.6 and 9.1*
